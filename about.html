Simona Pop

Romanian-born British entrepreneur, angel investor, governance strategist, community builder, conference producer, facilitator, and public speaker working at the intersection of decentralized governance, public goods, open coordination, and human flourishing.

Over the past decade she has helped build institutions, communities, governance systems, conferences, and funding mechanisms across the Ethereum ecosystem. Rather than focusing on any single organization, her work has consistently explored how people can coordinate more effectively, build resilient communities, and design healthier internet cultures.
