Hosted by Simona Pop

Nine years in crypto, burnout, and a lot of therapy later — this podcast exists. Human Protocol came from realizing that the conversations that actually changed something for me were never the polished ones — they were the raw ones, when someone finally said what they were really going through. This podcast is those conversations, recorded.

Follow @sim_pop Follow the show

Simona Pop

Romanian-born British entrepreneur, governance strategist, community builder, conference producer, facilitator, and public speaker working at the intersection of decentralized governance, public goods, open coordination, and human flourishing.

Over the past decade she has helped build institutions, communities, governance systems, conferences, and funding mechanisms across the Ethereum ecosystem. Rather than focusing on any single organization, her work has consistently explored how people can coordinate more effectively, build resilient communities, and design healthier internet cultures.
